A Journey of Taste

Best authentic and delicious Indian cuisine in the Bay area. We offer the best in Pakistani and Indian food, making this a great place to enjoy your favorite standard ethnic meal or perhaps try a new one. From Chicken Korma to Lamb Curry and Chicken Tikka Masala, we deliver on taste, reasonable prices, friendly and accommodating service. Accept no substitutes when it comes to fine Indian cuisine, cooked mild or spicy all to your liking and your palette. Thinking of hosting a birthday party or event? We cater as well, so think of us for an event that is sure to be memorable.
